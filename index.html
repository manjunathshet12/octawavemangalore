About Octawave Technology

Octawave Technology is a VLSI training and internship provider focused on building strong fundamentals and practical skills in semiconductor design. We offer industry-oriented courses and training in VLSI Design.

Our programs are designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry requirements. Along with structured training, students are provided with internship opportunities and hands-on project experience.
